JBrowse

This page displays the genome-wide occupancy tracks of AtWRKYs, including data types of DAP, ampDAP, methyl-ampDAP, ChIP, and WGBS. Tracks named by tissues are DAP-seq libraries. DAP-seq-based libraries have two types of controls: "input" is the gDNA library before affinity purification, and the "TF-free" library captures the non-specific binding of Magne HaloTag Beads (affinity purification without TF expression).
